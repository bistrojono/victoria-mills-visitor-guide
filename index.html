Parking Instructions

  • Enter via Victoria Road, turning in next to the Victoria Mills sign
  • This leads directly into the surface visitor car park
  • Park in any available space between the white lines
  • Do not park outside marked bays (strictly enforced)

Important Parking Rules

  • 3 hours free parking (single continuous stay)
  • No leaving and re-entering on the same day
  • Parking is monitored via ANPR (automatic number plate recognition)
  • Failure to comply may result in a £100 charge
